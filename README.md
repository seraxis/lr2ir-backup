# LR2IR Backup

This is a backup that was attempted starting the 27th of May 2026 in response to [this tweet from the maintainer of the LR2IR](https://x.com/kenjidct/status/2059644318596661391) announcing the permanent discontinuation of service effective on the 31st of May 2026.

This attempts to back up all of the publically available data from LR2IR for archival purposes and use in it's successors.

## What is LR2IR

LR2IR (http://www.dream-pro.info/~lavalse/LR2IR/search.cgi) is a internet ranking platform designed in tandem with the BMS player Lunatic Rave 2, it is one of the earliest internet ranking sites that caught on and BMS popularised itself largely due to the existence of LR2 and it's IR.

The IR tracked over 350,000 charts, 130,000 players, and over 25 million scores. Each chart and player were given their own page with self authored descriptions along with other information. It remains one of the largest centralised repositories for BMS charts, players, and supplemental information, and many of it's features are not replicated in internet rankings that have come since.
